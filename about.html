Our Story

Hospitality, elevated.

Debbie's was born from a single belief — that travellers, families, businesses and celebrants in Abuja deserve a world-class home away from home.

Who we are

A landmark of comfort, style & hospitality

Nestled on the green hills of Orozo — at the gateway between Abuja and Nasarawa — Debbie's Suites & Apartments is a premium 3-star establishment combining serviced hotel apartments, a world-class event centre, two restaurants, a gym, indoor and outdoor lounges, and twin swimming pools.

Operating as Debbie's Upperclass Events & Recreation for corporate and celebration bookings, we host everything from intimate weddings to conferences of over a thousand guests — all with the meticulous care of a family-run estate.

Comfort, style, hospitality — these are not slogans. They are the standards by which every guest experience is measured.
